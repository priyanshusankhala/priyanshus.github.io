I am a visiting researcher at Cognitive Learning for Vision and Robotics Lab (CLVR) at University of Southern California, advised by Prof. Joseph Lim.

Previously, I spent 3 years working as the Lead Machine Learning Engineer of Documents AI at HyperVerge, Inc. My work used Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing for robust and scalable Information Extraction from real-world documents belonging to several countries.

I did my undergrad in Computer Science at Pune Institute of Computer Technology (University of Pune). Around this time, I interned at ITC InfoTech to solve problems in Face AI using Computer Vision.

Research

My research interest is to build machines that are able to perceive and act on multimodal information in their environments. I am interested in the intersection of the fields of Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing and Reinforcement Learning. I want to focus on learning representations from various modalities such as vision & language to enable agents to learn new tasks efficiently.
